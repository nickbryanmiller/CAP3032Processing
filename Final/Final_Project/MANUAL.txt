Instructions:

The goal is to dodge all of the enemies.
You do this by using the directional keypad.
Left moves you left.
Right moves right.
Up moves you up.
Down moves you down.

If you touch an enemy you lose one of your hearts at the top right.
If you lose three hearts the game is over.

You may select any difficulty to play.
These settings only change the images, music, and the speed of the enemies.

At any time you can back out of the game.
If you die you can restart.
